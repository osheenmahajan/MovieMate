Aashiqui 2

Rating and Description

"Aashiqui 2" is a poignant romantic drama that tells the story of Rahul, a fading singer, and Aarohi, an aspiring talent. Directed by Mohit Suri, the film delves into the complexities of love, ambition, and sacrifice. With soulful music and heartfelt performances by Aditya Roy Kapur and Shraddha Kapoor, the movie strikes an emotional chord with the audience. The soundtrack, especially songs like "Tum Hi Ho" and "Sunn Raha Hai," became instant hits, adding to the film's appeal. While the story treads on familiar territory, its execution and emotional depth make it a memorable watch.
