Andhadhun

Rating and Description

"Andhadhun," directed by Sriram Raghavan, is a captivating thriller that follows the story of a blind pianist, Akash, played by Ayushmann Khurrana. As Akash navigates his life and career, he becomes entangled in a web of murder and deceit after witnessing a crime. The film cleverly combines dark humor with unexpected twists, keeping viewers guessing at every turn. With stellar performances and a gripping narrative, "Andhadhun" explores themes of perception, morality, and the consequences of choices, making it a must-watch for thriller enthusiasts.
