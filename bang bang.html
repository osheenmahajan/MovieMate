Bang Bang

Rating and Description

"Bang Bang" is a high-octane action film starring Hrithik Roshan and Katrina Kaif. Directed by Siddharth Anand, this Bollywood adaptation of the Hollywood film "Knight and Day" brings together fast-paced action, dazzling stunts, and sizzling chemistry between the leads. The story follows a mysterious man on a mission, who teams up with a woman unwittingly drawn into his dangerous world of intrigue. With breathtaking visuals and heart-pounding chase scenes, "Bang Bang" delivers an entertaining rollercoaster ride for action lovers.
